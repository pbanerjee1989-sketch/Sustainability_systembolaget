# Sustainability Analysis of Systembolaget
## Project Overview

This project analyzes sustainability practices and performance indicators related to Systembolaget, Sweden’s state-owned alcohol retail monopoly. Systembolaget operates under a strong public responsibility mandate, with sustainability embedded across environmental, social, and governance (ESG) dimensions.
The goal of this project was to explore sustainability-related data to assess Systembolaget’s environmental impact, responsible consumption efforts, and supplier accountability. Python was used to clean, analyze, and interpret the data, with a focus on generating meaningful insights rather than visual storytelling.

## Dataset

- Source: Sustainability-related data inspired by Systembolaget’s public sustainability reporting
- Records: Multi-year sustainability observations
- Features include:
    - Environmental impact indicators (emissions, packaging, logistics)
    - Supplier sustainability metrics
    - Product-level sustainability attributes
    - Operational and reporting variables
- Target Focus: Sustainability performance and trends (descriptive & analytical, not predictive)

## Methodology

- Data loading and inspection
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Handling missing values and inconsistencies
- Feature transformation and categorization
- Descriptive analysis of sustainability indicators
- Interpretation of sustainability trends and impacts
- Insight generation aligned with ESG objectives

## Key Insights

- Sustainability performance varies significantly across product categories and suppliers
- Packaging and logistics-related factors contribute substantially to environmental impact
- Products with stronger sustainability certifications show improved environmental indicators
- Supplier-level accountability plays a critical role in overall sustainability outcomes

## Analytical Focus

- Emphasis on responsible retailing and environmental impact reduction
- Analysis aligned with Systembolaget’s social responsibility mandate
- Insights framed to support policy-driven decision-making, not profit maximization

## Business & Policy Recommendations

- Strengthen sustainability requirements for high-impact suppliers
- Promote environmentally responsible packaging alternatives
- Increase transparency in sustainability reporting metrics
- Encourage consumer awareness around sustainable product choices

## Ethical & Social Considerations
